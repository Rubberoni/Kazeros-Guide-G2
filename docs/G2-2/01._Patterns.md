## <ins>Clones</ins>

üóíÔ∏è<strong>Explanation</strong> 
<p align=justify>Kazeros spawns 8 clones - 3 of the same colour and 5 of the other. Safe spot will be the clones which count is smaller, while the others will thrust forward. After that orb will spawn inside of the clones shoting rotating beams from both sides. Then Kazeros will appear with a lots of 2 coloured cuts around the map.</p> 

<video width="800" height="500" controls="" muted="">
    <source src="https://github.com/Rubberoni/Kazeros-G2-Clips/raw/refs/heads/main/G2-2%20Clips/G2-2_Clones.mp4">
</video>

‚úÖ<strong>Useful information</strong>
<p align=justify>If you managed to hide in the safe spot you can ignore the cuts - they wont do anything to you and its a free dps window. If you stood on the wrong one then you will get sent flying and take damage from the cuts. If you push immune you wont get sent flying, but you will still take damage from the cuts. Supports should shield/DR people who failed this mech.</p>
<p align=justify>There is another way to differentiate which clone is safe - If there are 2 clones of the same colour next to each other this means that colours is not safe. It also helps with there being only 2 variations which looks exactly the same with only the colour being changed.</p>

## <ins>6 hit combo</ins>

üóíÔ∏è<strong>Explanation</strong> 
<p align=justify>Deadly pattern that targets one person with 6 hits. Order of the motions is:</p> 

<p align=justify>Front cleave -> Front cleave -> Thrust -> Pull in -> Uppercut -> Slash</p> 

<video width="800" height="500" controls="" muted="">
    <source src="https://github.com/Rubberoni/Kazeros-G2-Clips/raw/refs/heads/main/G2-2%20Clips/G2-2_5_Hit_Combo.mp4">
</video>

‚úÖ<strong>Useful information</strong>
<p align=justify>His 5 initial hits are guaranteed to target the same player, but the last one is based on something else - who got hit by the 'pull in'. Any players that got hit by it can be targeted by the last slash, which is important to control aggro on this one and stay on the front if you are the one being targeted.</p>
<p align=justify>If the boss did rotate during this pattern and multiple people got hit by the 'pull in' make sure to keep him looking towards the same direction. Other than that be ready for that person to get targeted.</p> 
<p align=justify>You need para immunity for first 4 hits and push immunity for last 2. Supports should DR this pattern if they can.</p>
