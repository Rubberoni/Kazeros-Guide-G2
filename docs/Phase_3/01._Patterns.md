# <ins>Normal Patterns</ins>

<p align=justify>This section explains normal patterns that Kazeros uses in Phase 3.  
There are new ones as well as enhanced or changed versions of previous ones.</p>

<p align=justify>This Phase is extremely dangerous and the hardest out of all 3 so you want to make sure that you have as much time as possible in this gate. There are lots of downtime patterns</p>

<p align=justify>This phase reintroduces <code style="color : Purple">gauge</code> again with the same <code style="color : Purple">special patterns</code> and 1 new.  
It changes how it behaves, by never resetting after filling up to full and instead staying for the rest of the fight.  
That means he will be able to do <code style="color : Purple">special patterns</code> as his normal ones, BUT it only applies to <code style="color : Purple">X</code>. That's the only one that he starts using from that point.</p>

<p align=justify>There is also one thing (which might be a bug, who knows). He can do <code style="color : Purple">X</code> as his normal pattern sometimes, not related to <code style="color : Purple">gauge</code> BEFORE he reaches 100%. It rarely happens, but it is a thing. Beware of that if it doesn't get changed.</p>

## <ins>Tornado charge</ins>

<p align=justify>Kazeros spawns tornadoes on 4 different players and sends a singular line of explosions forward. After that he dashes backwards while also sending 6 additional lines, 3 per side in a 60 degree cone. Then he <code style="color : Red">aggroes</code> onto one person and thrust him with his spear.</p>

<video width="800" height="500" controls="" muted="">
    <source src="https://github.com/Rubberoni/Gifs/raw/refs/heads/main/G1/Phase_3/Tornado_Charge_VID.mp4">
</video>

<i><p align=center>He locks on the target and after a bit stops following him. You can move out of the way if you are fast or just spacebar to be safe.  
There is also an indicator showing if you are the one being targeted just like during 'Aggro Guard', but currently sometimes it doesn't show. If it's not fixed till our release beware of that.</p></i>

## <ins>Xayah</ins>

<p align=justify>Kazeros <code style="color : Red">aggroes</code> one person and starts sending vertical slashes toward him. At the same time at the end of their travel (or if the player gets hit) it spawns the shadow.  
After the final slash Kazeros will do one more horizontal slash with a larger hitbox followed by attacks from all shadows towards the targeted person (this one is unsure, check lower).  
You might remember a similar version of this pattern from Thaemine, when he disappears and does a slash followed by attacks from all of his clones.</p>

## <ins>Line</ins>

<p align=justify>Kazeros throws a spear into the ground in front of him and makes a line which explodes, that goes through his front and back.  
He then follows up with expanding lines towards his sides while also requiring stagger.  
After the stagger he will do 180-200 side cleave on his right side. His left side being the safe spot.  
Its very similar pattern to Act 2 G1 when he slams his back with his tail, followed up by explosions going sideways.</p>

<p align=justify>Kazeros makes a <code style="color : Darkorange">guard</code> by charging forward and disappears while spawning 6 clones charging outwards from his position. Then he reappears behind 1 player and cleaves in front.</p>

## <ins>Zig-Zag</ins>

<p align=justify>Kazeros dashes right and left alternating between those 2 aiming at the middle.  
Whenever he hits a person with one of his dashes it will spawn blades on top of him that go outwards.  
His final dash also ends up in an aoe explosion on top of him.</p>
