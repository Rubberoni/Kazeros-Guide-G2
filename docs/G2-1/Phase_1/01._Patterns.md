# <ins>Normal Patterns</ins>

<p align=justify>This sections explains normal patterns that Kazeros uses during phase 1 till the broken arena.</p>

## <ins>Wall</ins>

<p align=justify>This pattern has 3 different variations while also being a follow-up to a few other patterns (which im gonna mention while explaining them).</p>

<p align=justify>1. Front wall</p>

<video width="800" height="500" controls="" muted="">
    <source src="https://github.com/Rubberoni/Kazeros-G2-Clips/raw/refs/heads/main/G2-1%20Clips/G2-1_Start_Wall.mp4">
</video>

<i><p align=center>Its his starting pattern on this gate.  
He does a backdash and creates a wall in front of him.</i></p>

<p align=justify>2. Back wall</p>

<video width="800" height="500" controls="" muted="">
    <source src="https://github.com/Rubberoni/Kazeros-G2-Clips/raw/refs/heads/main/G2-1%20Clips/G2-1_Back_Wall.mp4">
</video>

<i><p align=center>He stands in place and creates a wall behind him.</i></p>

<p align=justify>3. Side wall</p>

<video width="800" height="500" controls="" muted="">
    <source src="https://github.com/Rubberoni/Kazeros-G2-Clips/raw/refs/heads/main/G2-1%20Clips/G2-1_Middle_Wall.mp4">
</video>

<i><p align=center>He stands in place and creates walls on the both side with front/back safe.</i></p>

<i><p align=center>To differentiate this pattern you need to look at his hands. Red glowing hands with side motion indicates the pattern while the follow-up motion indicates the variation.  
Front wall - always starts with a back dash (easiest one)  
Back wall - he spreads his hands 2 times  
Side wall - he spreads his hands 1 time</i></p>
