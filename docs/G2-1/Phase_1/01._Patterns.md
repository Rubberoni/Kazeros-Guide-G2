# <ins>Normal Patterns</ins>

<p align=justify>This sections explains normal patterns that Kazeros uses during phase 1 till the broken arena.</p>
<p align=justify>You will also see that some patterns will have something that i call 'after-hit'. What i mean by that is Kazeros on some patterns will follow-up his hits with a burst of air that deals damage and also pushes you (which can be ignored with at least para immunity).</p>

## <ins>Jump Guard</ins>

üóíÔ∏è<strong>Explanation</strong> 
<p align=justify>Kazeros kneels and a red aura surrounds him. After a moment he jumps, creating an explosion around him, and dashes towards one player after he lands. Then he performs a <code style="color : Darkorange">guard</code> towards him followed by a cleave. This pattern has 2 variations based on if anyone got hit by the cleave.</p>

<p align=justify>1. Jump guard into back wall</p>

<p align=justify>If anyone got hit by the cleave he will then spawn a wall behind him.</p>

<video width="800" height="500" controls="" muted="">
    <source src="https://github.com/Rubberoni/Kazeros-G2-Clips/raw/refs/heads/main/G2-1%20Clips/G2-1_Jump_Guard_Back_Wall.mp4">
</video>

<p align=justify>2. Jump guard into front wall</p>

<p align=justify>If no one got hit by the cleave he will then do a backdash and spawn a wall in front of him.</p>

<video width="800" height="500" controls="" muted="">
    <source src="https://github.com/Rubberoni/Kazeros-G2-Clips/raw/refs/heads/main/G2-1%20Clips/G2-1_Jump_Guard_Front_Wall.mp4">
</video>

‚úÖ<strong>Useful information</strong>

<p align=justify>He doesnt deal damage when he lands.</p>
<p align=justify><code style="color : Darkorange">Guard</code> is a ~180 angle on his right side which means you can ignore it completely on his left side. Cleave is a ~270 angle covering both of his sides + front which means back is safe.</p>
<p align=justify>You can also completely ignore this <code style="color : Darkorange">guard</code> using any skill with at least para immunity, because it doesnt affect his cleave and neither the wall. Wall spawn is always based on somebody getting hit by the cleave.</p>
<p align=justify>If you have lots of back attackers this pattern is a nice DPS window for them if nobody gets hit by the wall. If somebody does get hit by it make sure to call out/ping so the other can expect 'Back wall'.</p>

üóíÔ∏è<strong>Explanation</strong>

<p align=justify>Kazeros takes a step back and kicks one person which he later follows-up with a punch behind his back into an 'after-hit' and finishes off with a front punch into an 'after-hit'.</p>

‚úÖ<strong>Useful information</strong>

<p align=justify>All of the hits require only para immunity.</p>
<p align=justify>His 'after-hit' behind his back is a bit different compered to the others. This one instead of pushing you away from him will pull you towards his front.</p>
<p align=justify>His front punch knocks people up. If you are right in front of him during that he will the grab you, but if you are not close enough he will just push you away from him with his 'after-hit'.</p>

## <ins>Right/Left</ins>

üóíÔ∏è<strong>Explanation</strong>

<p align=justify>Kazeros does 2 cleaves with a different hands and then follows-up with an uppercut. AFter that he rotates and does a ~240 angle cleave in front of him. This pattern has 2 variations with an only difference between them being a hand that he starts the pattern with.</p>

<p align=justify>1. Left -> Right</p>

<p align=justify>He cleaves with his left hand and then right. Then he follows-up with a left hand uppercut.</p>

<p align=justify>2. Right -> Left</p>

<p align=justify>He cleaves with his right hand and then left. Then he follows-up with a right hand uppercut.</p>

<p align=justify>His last cleave has also a safespot right in front of the boss which can be useful for front attackers or if you cant make it behind him.</p>

## <ins>Double Guard</ins>

üóíÔ∏è<strong>Explanation</strong>

<p align=justify>Kazeros targets one person and dashes through him. Then he cleaves him and does a <code style="color : Darkorange">guard</code> followed by another one behind him.</p>

<video width="800" height="500" controls="" muted="">
    <source src="https://github.com/Rubberoni/Kazeros-G2-Clips/raw/refs/heads/main/G2-1%20Clips/G2-1_Double_Guard.mp4">
</video>

‚úÖ<strong>Useful information</strong>
<p align=justify>Initial dash doesnt do anything - its only for Kazeros to reach the player.</p>
<p align=justify>His <code style="color : Darkorange">guard</code> is a ~140 angle which means if you are close to his side you wont have to <code style="color : Darkorange">guard</code>. You also have to <code style="color : Darkorange">guard</code> only one time, unless the person Kazeros was targeting moved too far away from him just before the <code style="color : Darkorange">guard</code>. The reason for that is Kazeros is dashing with his <code style="color : Darkorange">guard</code>, so he can accidentaly end up behind you, which forces you to perform 2 <code style="color : Darkorange">guards</code> instead of 1. Thats why its important to not run away from him during this pattern.</p>
<p align=justify>His initial cleave requires only para immune. You can also just ignore the <code style="color : Darkorange">guard</code> by using any skills with push immune which is a good way to greed with shields from supports.</p>

## <ins>Triple Jump</ins>

üóíÔ∏è<strong>Explanation</strong>

<p align=justify>Kazeros jumps in place, targets one person and then slides towards him dealing damage in front. After that he does an uppercut behind followed by an 'after-hit' and slams down, dealing damage around and creating blue lines which stun people after a moment and explode a bit later. Then he does an uppercut behind him again, also followed by an 'after-hit' and finishes this time with an explosion where he lands followed by an explosion outside.</p>

<video width="800" height="500" controls="" muted="">
    <source src="https://github.com/Rubberoni/Kazeros-G2-Clips/raw/refs/heads/main/G2-1%20Clips/G2-1_Triple_Jump.mp4">

‚úÖ<strong>Useful information</strong>
<p align=justify>He always follows one person, which means he can also change direction between the jumps.</p>
<p align=justify>You can partially greed on his back, because there is no hitbox till he does an uppercut.</p>
<p align=justify>In hardmode there is no explosion on top of him on his last landing, so you can stick to his back. There is only an explosion around. </p>

üóíÔ∏è<strong>Explanation</strong>

<p align=justify>Kazeros does a backflip and targets 1 player. After a moment he sends multiple waves of slashes towards that person followed by one final wave in X's shape.</p>

You should just spacebar/push immune it and supports should DR it. If you dont have anything up just make sure to not send it into your whole team and tank it with a support's help.</p>

üóíÔ∏è<strong>Explanation</strong>

<p align=justify>Kazeros's right hand glows red. He then jumps and drops on top of one person. After he lands he will instantly to grab him. This pattern has 4 variations with different outcomes.</p>

<p align=justify>1. Grab missed.</p>

<p align=justify>Kazeros does nothing.</p>

<p align=justify>2. Grab connected and you succeeded the <code style="color : Fuchsia">co-op counter</code>.</p>

<p align=justify>Kazeros slams the ground with the person he grabbed 4 times dealing damage around. After the 4th slam he will perform the <code style="color : Fuchsia">co-op counter</code>. If you hit it, he will then follow-up with either 'Back wall' or 'Front wall'.</p>

<p align=justify>Kazeros slams the ground with the person he grabbed 4 times dealing damage around. After the 4th slam he will perform the <code style="color : Fuchsia">co-op counter</code>. If you miss it he will then do one more slam with a larger radius.</p>
