# <ins>Patterns</ins>

<p align=justify>Phase 3, just like phase 2, is a sequence of patterns that repeat till you kill Kazeros.</p>

<p align=justify>A few things worth mentioning: multiple patterns in this phase inflicts the debuff that makes you eplode after a few seconds (red fog from 'Imprison' mech and a few other patterns) and one that reduces your healing received (this one stacks up to 5 times and then transforms into 100% reduced healing received - this one is not cleansable and lasts for ~2 minutes).</p>

## <ins>Transition</ins>

<p align=justify>After you finish the last clash in phase 2 Kazeros will activate a cutscene and transition into another arena. He lands in a middle and sends 3 waves of blue lines. On the 3rd wave he also spawns puddles under all players. After that he will spawn 4 cones and additional red line under each player. This is also a timing for bonus clash which can be taken by the closest player. Its not a mandatory to take that one, but it is an additional damage opening. After that he ascends and lands creating an explosion which instakill the players.</p>

## <ins>Tile mech</ins>

<p align=justify>After that Kazeros transform arena into a 6x6 square and players split into x3/x3+1 positions. One random player will be targeted by a <code style="color : Darkorange">guard</code> while others will destroy 2 tiles around them. After the succesful <code style="color : Darkorange">guard</code> Kazeros will land on top of that player and do a <code style="color : Fuchsia">co-op counter</code>.</p>

<video width="800" height="500" controls="" muted="">
    <source src="https://github.com/Rubberoni/Kazeros-G2-Clips/raw/refs/heads/main/G2-1%20Clips/G2-1_Tile_Mech.mp4">
</video>

<i><p align=center>If you fail <code style="color : Darkorange">guard</code> Kazeros will repeat the pattern ultimately leaving you with no space to resolve the mech if you fail it repeatedly.    
It is advised to gather middle and the person who was targeted should be close, but not close enough to overlap Kazeros explosion with the other players.  
The trick to know if you are the one being targeted is by seeing if camera zoomed in. If it did that means its either you or the person close to you.  
If you are not the one being targeted you want to use any skill with at least para immunity. Sometimes Kazeros might hit you by targeting person close to you which might push you off the map.</i></p>

<p align=justify>After that he will vanish and dash 2 times towards the random players performing a <code style="color : Darkorange">guard</code>.</p>

## <ins>Twin swords - specials</ins>

<p align=justify>After Kazeros performs 3 of these patterns he will use one of his 2 Special Patterns exclusive to this phase.</p>

<p align=justify>1.Co-op counter</p>

<p align=justify>Kazeros will do double 'Pole vault' pattern in one place followed by a <code style="color : Fuchsia">co-op counter</code>. After that he will explode 2 times with inside safe every time and finish with dash spin towards the players.<p>

<p align=justify>2.Guard</p>

<p align=justify>Kazeros will start with a backflip into 'Pole vault'. He will then spin around him and explode outside with inside safe. ## <ins>Rectangle arena</ins>

<p align=justify>After that he will dash forward and destroy large chunk of arena behind him. Then he will do a backstep and jump towards towards the part which he destroyed, with the safespot being right in front of it, sending all of the players flying. After that players will have to do QTE (button A) and position themselves in a way to avoid his attack, which is a line going through him.</p>

<video width="800" height="500" controls="" muted="">

<i><p align=center>Make sure to quickly move out from the parts of the arena he destroys to not fall off the map.  
Quickly identify safepot during the QTE by checking where the boss is facing and dash toward the side.</i></p>

## <ins>DPS check</ins>

<p align=justify>At this point boss recovers parts of the arena and gets additional bar under his health. You have to deal enough dmg during 1 minute to make it dissapear. During this phase boss uses all his normal patterns from phase 1 as well as special patterns from >75% meter every 2-3 normal patterns (enough time for only 1).</p>

## <ins>DPS check 2</ins>

<p align=justify>Final mech of this phase. Boss will return to the 'Tiled arena' and 3 small cones coming out of him will appear. By standing inside of them you can fill them up making them dissapear after dealing enough damage.</p>

<video width="800" height="500" controls="" muted="">
    <source src="https://github.com/Rubberoni/Kazeros-G2-Clips/raw/refs/heads/main/G2-1%20Clips/G2-1_Last_DPS_Check.mp4">

<i><p align=center>You want to split into 3 groups of 2 to quickly get rid of them. You should have Hyper awakening saved for that mech (you can also use kadan to destroy one of the cones).  
You cant move out of them otherwise the bar will reset.  
Not getting rid of them will result in raid wipe.</i></p>
