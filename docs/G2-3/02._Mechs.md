## <ins>Sacrifice(40%)</ins>

üóíÔ∏è<strong>Explanation</strong> 
<p align=justify>Kazeros will spawn an orb far away in front of him. One person has to enter it to activate a stagger mech. After that he will slowly walk towards him while exploding around. You have to stagger him before he reaches that person.</p>

<p align=justify>Explosion -> explosion -> <code style="color : Darkorange">guard</code> -> explosion -> <code style="color : Fuchsia">co-op counter</code> -> explosion -> explosion.</p>

## <ins>Rotating pizza (0% < x < 25%)</ins>

üóíÔ∏è<strong>Explanation</strong> 
<p align=justify>This one seems simple on paper, but execution gets a little bit tricky.</p>

<p align=justify>At the start Kazeros will split arena into 2 diferent colours. The colour you get hit by is the one which will be your safe spot for the whole mech.</p>

<p align=justify>After that he will start spawning cones of 2 different colours and you have to avoid the opposite one. After 2 explosions he will change position of arena splits. He will repeat this sequence 2 times and then he will start rotating as well.</p>

<video width="800" height="500" controls="" muted="">
    <source src="https://github.com/Rubberoni/Kazeros-G2-Clips/raw/refs/heads/main/G2-3%20Clips/G2-3_Fake_Death.mp4">
</video>

<p align=justify>Now here comes the tricky part. At the start of this mech he split arena into 2 colours. It did 2 things:</p>

* Marked you with the colours you need to stick with
* Showed you on which part of the arena you have to stay

[last mech](https://youtu.be/hVMtggRqIik?si=pTPQXgLlnpc8tzsK&t=1741) - clip because the whole mech is too long and quality will be too bad to see what's happening.

<p align=justify>You can notice in the clip, that the halfed arenas did not dissapear. They are always there, but hidden bellow the pizza cones. That means you not only have to stick with the cone that has your starting colour. You also have to make sure that you stay on your half of the arena. Now where is the tricky part?</p>

<p align=justify>During the rotation.</p>

<p align=justify>Your safe half will be rotating around the Kazeros while he also spawns cones around. That means you have to rotate with it constantly and also lookout for the safe cones. Not only that - during this whole mech you have to attack him. It wont end otherwise.</p>

<p align=justify>Some infographic:</p>

<p align=center><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Rubberoni/Kazeros-G2-Clips/main/G2-3%20Clips/Last%20mech.jpg"></p>

<p align=justify>If you started on blue, your safe spots will be on the left side on blue parts. If you started on yellow, your safe spots will be on the right side on yellow parts.</p>
<p align=justify>If you get hit then you receive a debuff that makes you die after 10 seconds. If that happens just try to deal as much damage as possible before you die. You cant survive it even with Crisis.</p>
