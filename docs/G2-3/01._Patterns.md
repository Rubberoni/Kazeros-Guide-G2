ğŸ—’ï¸<strong>Explanation</strong> 
<p align=justify>Kazeros dissapears and reappears with a <code style="color : Darkorange">guard</code> 2 times after which he targets 1 person and shoots out a projectiles towards him which explode into a lingering cross explosion if it connects.</p>

<video width="800" height="500" controls="" muted="">
    <source src="https://github.com/Rubberoni/Kazeros-G2-Clips/raw/refs/heads/main/G2-3%20Clips/G2-3_Blink_Guard.mp4">

âœ…<strong>Useful information</strong>
<p align=justify>Make sure to see who he is targeting after guarding. Cross deals infinite damage in TFM and you can completely get rid of it by not getting hit with a projectile.</p>
<p align=justify>You want to stay bit further away from him and sidestep it the moment he locks on to your position. Make sure to not stand on top of him/right behind him either before he shoots it. Its hitbox reaches his back.</p>
